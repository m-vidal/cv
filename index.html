About me

MSc Math Stat | B. Comm | B. Music

I develop statistical methods and algorithms for preprocessing and analyzing medical imaging data (EEG, fMRI, pupillometry), combining elements of infinite-dimensional and high-dimensional data analysis. I focus on nonlinear normalization and independent component analysis techniques. My primary emphasis is on data-driven modeling and inference of neurotransmitter activity during states of motor and emotional arousal associated with embodiment processes.
