About me

MSc Math Stat | B. Comm | B. Music

Marc is a researcher in neuroscience and mathematical and applied statistics, promoted by Prof. Dr. M. Leman and Prof. Dr. A. Aguilera. He is interested in modelling biosignals (EEG, fMRI, pupillometry) to uncover the neuromodulatory effects of motor intention during music performance tasks. His research also focuses on functional data-based statistical techniques, particularly in independent component and dynamic systems models.

Research areas

Mathematical and data-driven models in embodied music neuroscience; Independent components techniques for high-dimensional and large-scale data; Pre-processing and inferential methods in neuroscience and related fields.
